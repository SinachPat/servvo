## Block 3 — How it works (3 steps)

1. **Connect** — Link Toast, Square, Clover, or 7shifts with one click each. You approve
   access on the vendor's own screen; we never see your passwords.
2. **Choose** — Pick which locations go live. Reads are on; anything that changes data
   is off until you switch it on.
3. **Ask** — Paste your Servvo address into the AI you use. Then just ask: *"Compare
   average check across all stores this month."*

## Block 9 — FAQ

- **Which AI does this work with?** Any assistant that supports the open MCP standard —
  Claude and a growing list. You're never locked to one AI vendor.
- **Can it change prices or refund customers?** Only if you turn those on. Money
  actions are capped and always require a human confirmation. Everything is logged.
- **Do you see my card data?** No. We never store or process cardholder data; we read
  business data (sales totals, menus, schedules) through each vendor's official API.
- **What if my stores run different POS systems?** That's our favorite case — Servvo
  answers across a mixed Toast + Square estate as if it were one system.
- **How long does setup really take?** Under 30 minutes for most brands. Watch the
  unedited video.
- **What happens if I cancel?** One click disconnects every system; we revoke our
  access with each vendor and purge your credentials.
